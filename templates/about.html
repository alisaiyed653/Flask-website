Welcome to Stone Choice Properties

At Stone Choice Properties, we blend expertise with a deep-rooted understanding of the local area, ensuring a seamless experience for both landlords and tenants alike. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every stage of our process.

Our Process
1st Stage: Procurement

We begin by diligently sourcing properties that meet our high standards and align with the preferences of our clients. Leveraging our extensive knowledge of the area, we identify prime locations and properties with potential for rental success. Once a suitable property is found, we negotiate with the landlord to agree on rent terms, ensuring a fair and beneficial arrangement for all parties involved. Our leases typically span a minimum of 6 months, providing stability and peace of mind to both landlords and tenants.

2nd Stage: Refurbishment

To uphold our commitment to quality, we undertake minor refurbishments on selected properties. Our team of skilled professionals meticulously refurbishes each property, ensuring it meets the standards that our discerning tenants expect. From fresh coats of paint to essential repairs, we spare no effort in transforming properties into inviting homes.

3rd Stage: Rental Management

Once leased, we manage the property with precision and care. Rent payments are processed promptly every month, providing landlords with a reliable source of income. Moreover, we conduct regular inspections to ensure that the property is well-maintained and adheres to our high standards. Detailed inspection reports, complete with photographs, are promptly sent to landlords, offering assurance that their property is in optimal condition.

Peace of Mind: With our meticulous approach to property management, landlords can enjoy peace of mind knowing that their investment is in capable hands.
